## Common Questions â“

**Q: What if I solve multiple problems in one day?**
A: Great! But your streak only increases once per day. The total problems solved still counts toward the leaderboard.

**Q: What time does the day reset?**
A: The system uses midnight (00:00) in your local timezone as the day boundary.

**Q: Can I see my streak history?**
A: Currently, you can see your current streak and longest streak. Detailed history may come in future updates!

**Q: What happens if I re-solve a problem in a different language?**
A: Your solution is saved, but it doesn't count as a new problem for leaderboard ranking. Your streak still updates!

**Q: Does the leaderboard reset?**
A: No, it's all-time rankings based on total problems solved.
